### Banner Ads (Bottom of Screen)
**What users see:**
- Small ad at bottom of gallery screen
- Always visible while swiping
- Doesn't interrupt experience

**How you earn:**
- Paid per 1,000 views (CPM = Cost Per Mille)

- Immediately when user opens gallery
- Changes every 30-60 seconds
- Different ads for different users

### Rewarded Video Ads (Bonus Swipes)
**What users see:**
- "Watch ad for 5 bonus swipes" button
- 15-30 second video ad
- Get reward after watching

**How you earn:**
- Paid per completed view
- Average: $1 - $5 per 1,000 views
- Example: 1,000 users watching = $1-5

**Why they're better:**
- 10x higher revenue than banners!
- Users choose to watch (better experience)
- Higher engagement = more money
